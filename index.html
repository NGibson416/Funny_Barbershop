About

Sir Ignoramus The 3rd "The Patron Fellow Debutante Of Worcestershire" - My great great great grandfather's great great grandfather established our barbershop 7 generations ago. He was the first official hair stylist of the Debutante Ball in England, he is actually creadited with inventing the modern "Friar" referred to as the "Visor", which only covered the front and sides of the head to keep sweat from falling on the debutantes faces due to lack of air conditioning (A/C) during those balls.
